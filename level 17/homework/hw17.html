khachapuri

imeruli khachhapuri is one of the most famous georgian food thats popular and loved by tousrists

recipe

ingredients :

  • 500 g flour
  • 250 ml warm water
  • 1 ts salt
  • 1 ts yeast
  • 1 ts shugar
  • 400 g cheese
  • 1 egg

baking steps :

1 mix yeast and shugar in warm water and wait for 10 minutes

2 add flour and salt then mix the dough and wait for 1 hour

3 meanwhile mix cheese and salt

4 flatten dough and put mixed cheese inside

5 bake it on 200°C for 20-25 minute till outer part becomes golden color

facts and advices

alternative ingredients: you can use feta or other soft cheese instead of georgian cheese thats special for khachapuri.

story: khachapuri used to be symbol of Georgian cuisineand there are difrent variations of it in diffrent regions
